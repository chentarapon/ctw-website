About CTW (English)

CHAROENTAWEE WELDING (CTW) is a trusted supplier of TATA steel bars and standard construction steel in Thailand. We also provide factory construction services for small to large projects, including road and civil works such as concrete roads, factory yards, and site improvement works. Our focus is on quality, reliability, and on-time delivery to support construction projects of all scales.
